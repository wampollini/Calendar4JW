Calendar4JW

Smart Calendar Management for Jehovah's Witnesses

📅 What is Calendar4JW?

Calendar4JW is a specialized calendar application designed for Jehovah's Witnesses to efficiently manage congregation events, ministry schedules, circuit assembly dates, and personal appointments. The app seamlessly integrates with Google Calendar to provide bidirectional synchronization, ensuring your schedule is always up to date across all your devices.

✨ Key Features

🔄 Google Calendar Sync

Automatically sync events between Calendar4JW and your Google Calendar. Changes made in either place are reflected instantly.

📱 Multi-Platform

Access your calendar from any device - Android app or web browser. Your data syncs seamlessly across all platforms.

🎯 Event Types

Organize events by type: congregation meetings, circuit assemblies, regional conventions, memorial, CO visits, and personal appointments.

⏰ Smart Notifications

Set custom reminders for important events. Never miss a meeting or ministry appointment again.

📊 Service Hours

Track your ministry hours and Bible studies directly within your calendar.

🌍 Multi-Language

Available in English, Italian, and Spanish (more languages coming soon).

🔐 Why Google Calendar Access?

Calendar4JW requests access to your Google Calendar to:

  • Read your calendars - To display your existing events alongside congregation events
  • Create events - To add new meetings, assemblies, and ministry appointments
  • Edit events - To update event details, times, and locations
  • Delete events - To remove cancelled or rescheduled events
  • Sync bidirectionally - To keep Calendar4JW and Google Calendar always in sync

Your privacy matters: Calendar4JW only accesses calendar data. We never access your emails, contacts, or any other Google services. All data synchronization happens securely through Google's official APIs.
